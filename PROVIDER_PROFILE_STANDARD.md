# Provider Profile Standard

## Purpose

Standardize provider profiles across all SyncoraConnect verticals.

## Core Profile Fields

### Identity

* Provider Name
* Organization Name
* Provider Type
* Logo / Profile Image

### Classification

* Vertical
* Category
* Subcategory
* Service Tags

### Service Area

* City
* County
* State
* Virtual Services Available

### Contact Information

* Website
* Email
* Phone
* Social Media

### Statement of Work

Describe:

* Who you serve
* Problems you solve
* Services provided
* Geographic reach

### Experience

* Years of Experience
* Certifications
* Licenses
* Professional Associations

### Availability

* Accepting New Clients
* Waitlist
* Emergency Availability

### Verification

* Unverified
* Claimed
* Verified

### Reputation

* Reviews
* Ratings
* Endorsements

## MVP Rule

The Statement of Work is the most important profile field and should be displayed prominently.

## Future Enhancements

* AI-generated summaries
* Service guarantees
* Provider badges
* Community endorsements
* Portfolio and case studies

## Team Structure

- Total Team Members
- Professional Staff Count
- Support Staff Count
- Volunteer Count (if applicable)

## Team Roles

Examples:
- Attorneys
- Paralegals
- Administrative Staff
- Financial Advisors
- Insurance Agents
- Event Coordinators
- Volunteers
