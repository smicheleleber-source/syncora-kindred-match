# Business Model V1

## Purpose

Establish the initial revenue model for SyncoraConnect while maintaining trust, transparency, and high-quality matching.

## Customer Side

### Free

Customers may:

* Search for matches
* Complete Discovery
* Generate Match Briefs
* View provider recommendations

No cost to submit requests.

## Provider Side

### Free Provider Profile

Includes:

* Provider Profile
* Statement of Work
* Basic Matching
* Unverified Status

### Claimed Provider

Includes:

* Claimed Profile
* Profile Management
* Enhanced Visibility

### Verified Provider

Includes:

* Verification Badge
* Enhanced Trust Signals
* Future Ranking Benefits

## Future Subscription Tiers

### Starter

Ideal for solo providers.

### Professional

Ideal for small teams.

### Organization

Ideal for firms, nonprofits, and agencies.

## Premium Features

Potential future features:

* AI Match Insights
* Enhanced Analytics
* Lead Tracking
* Team Management
* Priority Placement
* Custom Discovery Workflows

## Marketplace Principles

### Matching Integrity

Providers may not purchase guaranteed placement.

Match quality remains the primary ranking factor.

### Transparency

Verification status must be visible.

Sponsored content must be identified.

### Provider Diversity

Solo providers and large organizations should both be able to compete effectively.

## Future Revenue Opportunities

* Provider subscriptions
* Verification fees
* Sponsored placement
* Event promotion
* Organization memberships
* Volunteer recruitment services
* Self Governance Navigation Center partnerships

## MVP Goal

Generate provider adoption first.

Revenue optimization occurs after marketplace liquidity is achieved.
