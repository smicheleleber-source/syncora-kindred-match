SyncoraConnect Discovery Engine
Purpose
The Discovery Engine gathers structured information before matching occurs.
The goal is to improve match quality and provide providers with a concise Match Brief.
Workflow
	1. User submits initial request.
	2. AI identifies vertical and category.
	3. Discovery questions are generated.
	4. User answers questions.
	5. AI creates Match Brief.
	6. Matching Engine scores providers.
	7. Provider receives Match Brief.
Supported Verticals
	• Legal Services
	• Events & Event Professionals
	• Financial Services
	• Insurance Networks
	• Political & Advocacy Alignment
	• Self Governance Navigation Center
	• Nonprofits & Volunteer Matching
