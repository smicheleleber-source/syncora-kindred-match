# Provider Onboarding V1

## Goal

Separate customers from providers.

Customers use:

Find My Match

Providers use:

Join Our Network of Providers

## Provider Entry Question

What type of provider are you?

## Provider Types

1. Professional Service Provider
- Attorney
- CPA
- Financial Advisor
- Insurance Agent
- Consultant
- Coach

2. Organization
- Nonprofit
- Community Organization
- Church
- Advocacy Group
- Civic Organization

3. Event Professional
- Event Planner
- Photographer
- DJ
- Venue
- Caterer

4. Volunteer Organization
- Volunteer Coordinator
- Service Project
- Community Outreach

5. Partner Organization
- Sponsor
- Business Partner
- Government Agency
- Strategic Partner

## Verification Status

Providers may appear in matches before full verification.

Statuses:

- unverified
- claimed
- verified

## MVP Rule

Unverified providers can still appear in matches.

They should display:

Profile Pending Verification

Verified providers should display:

Verified

## Future Matching Impact

Verified providers may receive ranking preference later.

Claimed providers may receive moderate ranking preference.

Unverified providers remain eligible but visibly marked.
